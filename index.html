Must-Do Experiences

New York

From iconic landmarks to vibrant culture, New York has it all.

  • Take in the view from the top of the Empire State Building
  • Walk through Central Park’s scenic paths
  • Experience Times Square at night
  • Explore the Metropolitan Museum of Art
  • Stroll through Brooklyn Bridge Park

California

California offers natural beauty, bustling cities, and outdoor adventures.

  • See the sunset at the Santa Monica Pier
  • Drive along the scenic Pacific Coast Highway
  • Hike in Yosemite National Park
  • Tour the vineyards in Napa Valley
  • Visit Disneyland in Anaheim

Florida

Known for beaches, amusement parks, and vibrant cities.

  • Relax on the sandy shores of Miami Beach
  • Visit the magical world of Disney at Walt Disney World
  • Explore the Everglades by airboat
  • Discover art and culture in Wynwood, Miami
  • Check out the unique wildlife at the Florida Keys

Texas

A state of rich history, diverse landscapes, and vibrant city life.

  • Tour The Alamo in San Antonio
  • Enjoy live music on Sixth Street in Austin
  • Explore the NASA Space Center in Houston
  • Stroll along the River Walk in San Antonio
  • Take in the views at Big Bend National Park

Illinois

Famous for Chicago, historic landmarks, and scenic routes.

  • Visit the Art Institute of Chicago
  • Take a photo at The Bean in Millennium Park
  • Go to the top of Willis Tower for sky-high views
  • Experience the nightlife on Chicago’s Magnificent Mile
  • Walk the historic Route 66

Atlanta

Georgia's capital, offering a blend of history, culture, and Southern charm.

  • Visit the Georgia Aquarium, home to thousands of marine animals
  • Explore the World of Coca-Cola and learn about the iconic beverage
  • Wander through the historic Martin Luther King Jr. National Historical Park
  • Catch a show at the Fox Theatre, a stunning architectural masterpiece
  • Stroll through Piedmont Park, Atlanta's urban oasis
